Breaking down tasks into smaller parts and setting achievable deadlines and goals can help maintain focus and motivation. It is crucial to establish practical targets and hold oneself accountable to achieve them.

To boost both physical and mental health, it's crucial to incorporate regular exercise, eat a well-balanced diet, get sufficient rest, manage stress through techniques like meditation, maintain social connections, and avoid harmful habits like smoking and excessive drinking. If mental health concerns persist, reaching out to a mental health professional is advisable. Implementing these lifestyle changes can have a positive impact on overall health.
